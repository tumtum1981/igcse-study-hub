* MOMENTS TEST 3

20 Multiple Choice Questions | Advanced Moments Problems

Question 1

A uniform plank is 4 m long and weighs 100 N. If it is pivoted 1 m from one end, what moment does the plank's weight create about the pivot?

A 200 Nm clockwise
B 100 Nm (weight acts at centre, 1 m from pivot)
C 400 Nm
D 0 Nm
Question 2

Three forces act on a beam: 20 N at 2 m (clockwise), 15 N at 3 m (anticlockwise), and 10 N at 0.5 m (anticlockwise). What is the resultant moment?

A 50 Nm anticlockwise
B 40 Nm clockwise
C 10 Nm clockwise
D 10 Nm anticlockwise
Question 3

A diving board is 3 m long and fixed at one end. A 600 N diver stands at the free end. What moment is created about the fixed end?

A 1800 Nm
B 200 Nm
C 603 Nm
D 900 Nm
Question 4

A painter's ladder is held by a person at the base. The ladder is 5 m long and weighs 200 N (acting at its centre). What moment must the person counteract about the base?

A 1000 Nm
B 200 Nm
C 500 Nm
D 40 Nm
Question 5

On a balanced seesaw, Person A (500 N) sits 2.4 m from the pivot. Person B sits 3 m from the pivot. What is Person B's weight?

A 625 N
B 400 N
C 360 N
D 750 N
Question 6

A metre rule balances at the 50 cm mark. When a 3 N weight is hung at the 90 cm mark, the balance point moves to 62 cm. What is the weight of the rule?

A 3 N
B 5 N
C 6 N
D 7 N
Question 7

A A smaller moment than if applied perpendicular
B A larger moment than if applied perpendicular
C The same moment regardless of angle
D No moment at all
Question 8

A crowbar 1.2 m long has its pivot 0.2 m from the load end. If a 60 N effort is applied at the other end, what load can be lifted?

A 12 N
B 72 N
C 300 N
D 360 N
Question 9

Two weights hang from a horizontal bar pivoted at its centre. A 4 N weight at 25 cm and a 5 N weight at what distance will balance?

A 31.25 cm
B 20 cm
C 12.5 cm
D 100 cm
Question 10

A nutcracker applies a force of 200 N at the handles, 15 cm from the pivot. If the nut is 3 cm from the pivot, what force is applied to the nut?

A 1000 N
B 40 N
C 600 N
D 3000 N
Question 11

A 200 N
B 400 N
C 500 N
D 600 N
Question 12

A steering wheel has a radius of 0.2 m. If a moment of 8 Nm is needed to turn it, what force must be applied to the rim?

A 1.6 N
B 8.2 N
C 40 N
D 0.025 N
Question 13

A uniform rod of length 80 cm and weight 2 N is pivoted at one end. A 6 N force acts downward at the other end. What is the total clockwise moment?

A 4.8 Nm
B 5.6 Nm
C 6.4 Nm
D 0.8 Nm
Question 14

A Perpendicular to the lever arm
B Parallel to the lever arm
C At 45 degrees to the lever arm
D Towards the pivot
Question 15

B 140 N
C 40 N
D 35 N

B 12.6 N
C 20 N
D 0.05 N
Question 17

B 2 m from pivot
C 4 m from pivot
D 0.5 m from pivot

A No, the moment produced is only 30 Nm

D Cannot determine without knowing bolt size
