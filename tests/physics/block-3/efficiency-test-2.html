20 Multiple Choice Questions | Efficiency and Wasted Energy

Question 1

What is a closed energy system?

A A system where no energy is transferred
B A system where energy is created
C A system where energy is transferred but mass cannot enter or leave
D A system where energy is destroyed
Question 2

A 0.40
B 0.25
C 0.15
D 0.60
Question 3

In most machines, wasted energy is transferred to the surroundings as:

A Light energy
B Thermal energy
C Nuclear energy
D Chemical energy
Question 4

A 8,000 J
B 100,000 J
C 9,200 J
D 92,000 J
Question 5

A generator produces 4,500 J of electrical energy from 15,000 J of chemical energy. What is the wasted energy?

A 10,500 J
B 4,500 J
C 19,500 J
D 15,000 J
Question 6

A Motor X wastes less energy than Motor Y
B Motor X converts more energy usefully
C Motor Y converts a greater proportion of input energy usefully
D Both motors waste the same amount of energy
Question 7

A 0.30
B 0.70
C 1.00
D 0.50
Question 8

A Because energy is always created during transfers
B Because energy is always destroyed during transfers
C Because all devices produce sound energy only
D Because some energy is always dissipated as thermal energy due to friction or resistance

A 65%
B 28.6%
C 35%
D 3.5%
Question 10

A 0.30
B 0.70
C 0.35
D 1.43
Question 11

A 6,000 J
B 2,000 J
C 32,000 J
D 200 J
Question 12

A fan heater converts electrical energy into thermal energy and kinetic energy (for the fan). If 1,200 J of electrical energy produces 1,080 J of thermal energy and 60 J of kinetic energy, what is the total useful energy and the wasted energy?

A Useful: 1,080 J, Wasted: 120 J
B Useful: 60 J, Wasted: 1,140 J
C Useful: 1,200 J, Wasted: 0 J
D Useful: 1,140 J, Wasted: 60 J

A Energy that has been destroyed
B Energy that has been concentrated into one place
C Energy that has spread out into the surroundings and is no longer useful
D Energy that has been converted into a more useful form

B 30%

D 168%

D 0.65
